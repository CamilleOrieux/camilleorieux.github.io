Macrocosmos

An identity for the Macrocosmos festival, which celebrates the harmony between the city and its biodiversity. My approach is based on the idea that the city is an abundant set of various components. By exploring modular play, I conceive each shape as a cell which by interacting with each other, gives rise to a harmonious whole.
